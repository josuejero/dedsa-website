International Solidarity Working Group Formation

At our recent convention, Delaware DSA voted to dissolve the existing Palestine Solidarity Working Group (PSWG) in favor of a broader International Solidarity Working Group (ISWG). This new working group extends our focus beyond Palestine to include issues such as anti-imperialism, sanctions, and international prisoners, while maintaining our commitment to Palestinian liberation.

Applications Now Open for ISWG Membership

Active Delaware DSA members are invited to apply for membership in the new International Solidarity Working Group. To qualify, you must:

  • Be an active member of Delaware DSA
  • Be a member of our Discord server
  • Complete the application form (available on Discord)

Please check our Discord server for more information about the application process and to access the application form.

Palestinian Nakba Cultural Commemoration

Join Delaware DSA, Black Mothers in Power, and Delawareans for Palestinian Human Rights (DelPHR) for our Palestinian Nakba Cultural Commemoration. The event will feature art, poetry, and music honoring Palestinian culture and remembering the Nakba (catastrophe).
